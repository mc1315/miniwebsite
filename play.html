How to Play:
  • Match any four words to form a category
  • Click 'submit' and see if your words were connected
    • If they were: and you made one of the four connections, congrats!
    • If they were: but you're one away, you're close, keep trying.
    • If they weren't: try a new set of words
  • Try making another group of four
  • Keep going until you've matched all four categories
Tips for Playing:
  • Go for easier connections first - as you remove more of the options it's easier to figure out the trickier categories
  • Don't be fooled by words sitting next to each other that don't actually go together - use the shuffle option
  • Common connections include: words with similar meanings, words with one letter changed, words that sound like something else
